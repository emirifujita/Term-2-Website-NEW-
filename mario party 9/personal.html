Personal Connection

When I was in second grade, my uncle bequeathed his Nintendo DS Lite to me. It came with a game chip that had a variation of games and I tried playing them all however, none of them really grabbed my attention. Mario Party 9 was an exception though. When I started playing, I couldn't stop. All of the minigames were so addicting and it was impossible for me to stop playing. I would usually play it for several hours until my mom would persistently tell me to stop.

I no longer have the console anymore, but if I still had one, the first thing I would definitely do on it is play Mario Party 9.
